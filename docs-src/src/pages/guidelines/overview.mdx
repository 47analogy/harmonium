<p class="Lead">A Design System for teams that move fast. Scalable design tokens, accessible markup, and a comprehensive component library allow for harmonious user experiences.</p>
<hr/>
<h3>Scalable</h3>
<p><strong>Good design is flexible.</strong> Create solutions that enable products to gracefully accommodate new features, markets,and  environments.</p>
<ul class="BulletedList">
  <li>Provide space for innovation and iteration</li>
  <li>Help others see the big picture and plan for scale</li>
  <li>Create cross-functional design solutions</li>
</ul>
<hr/>
<h3>Accessible</h3>
<p><strong>We’re stronger together.</strong> Consider how our designs can be accessible and inclusive to all, regardless of ability, culture, location, or quality of internet access.</p>
<ul class="BulletedList">
  <li>Maintain accessibility standards</li>
  <li>Build responsively</li>
  <li>Understand your audience and build for their needs</li>
</ul>
<hr/>
<h3>Consistent</h3>
<p><strong>Consistency breeds stability.</strong> Maintain consistent standards and create reusable patterns that allow for a predictable user environment.</p>
<ul class="BulletedList">
  <li>Reusable components (use what we have or add your new stuff to the library)</li>
  <li>Design tokens for easy transfer of design patterns across applications</li>
  <li>Minimize users’ cognitive loads and decision-making time</li>
  <li>Enable users to interact with our products confidently</li>
</ul>
<hr/>
<h3>Purposeful</h3>
<p><strong>Don’t only make something that works.</strong> Design for experiences that people love to have repeatedly. Create aesthetically pleasing offerings that carry a consistent meaning in their form and function.</p>
<ul class="BulletedList">
  <li>Focus on essential, functional design</li>
  <li>Be aware of the impact of visual design</li>
  <li>Reduce distractions</li>
</ul>
